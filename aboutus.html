Cloud Licenses and Services

Acestar Sdn. Bhd. is a leading holistic IT solutions provider in the region since its inception in 2008. The company specializes in Cloud Based Licenses, High Performance Workstations and Digital Workflow & Automation.

Acestar is Adobe’s Only Platinum Partner in Malaysia as well as a prominent partner to other multinational brands such as HP (Hewlett Packard), Lenovo, Apple and Microsoft to name a few.

Acestar served more than 4,000 clients across the education, enterprise, SME and public sector markets, having covered more than 70% of higher educational institutions in Malaysia. There has been more than 39,000 Adobe Creative Cloud Licenses deployed to date. Being the early first few to advocate cloud licensing, the company provides the best-in-class Adobe Creative Cloud implementation and workshops as value added service to its clients.

Setting ourselves far from the conventional, Acestar empowers your workforce and operation procedures by bringing together exciting automation and digitization intelligence via frictionless digital tools, helping companies build a super-efficient working ecosystem that would definitely improve your company’s operational performance, maximize resources and allow you to scale rapidly. Expect carefree, hassle free, and always in control, satiating your company’s immediate needs and future expectations.

The repertoire of Acestar's creative solutions extends to being Maxon’s distributor in Southeast Asia carrying 3D powerhouse applications like Cinema 4D, Redshift, Red Giant, Universe and Forger.

For professional training and certifications, Acestar is the appointed Certiport Sole Distributor in Malaysia, providing some of the industry employers' most highly demanded such as Adobe Certified Associate, Microsoft Office Specialist and more. Outstandingly, Acestar even paved the way for the country's creative multimedia industry having spearheaded notable Creative Workshops & Events, namely: Adobe Certified Associate (ACA) Championship Malaysia and SEA Connect Creative Conference.

Natalie Sit, the Group CEO continues to foresee new heights and great expansions hence TechCare and Saleslives were added into the business’s new ventures. One’s An IT-As-A-Service model, the other an advanced CRM tool respectively. Entrusted by Acestar’s formidable relationship with partners, as well as combined wisdom across cloud licensing, managed service, business software and high performing workstations, the homegrown innovations are now taking the IT industry by storm where both sets itself in a class of its own.

The next chapter of Acestar encapsulates – Simplicity. Scalability. Sustainability. Supportive.
